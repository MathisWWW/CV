Research Projects

Projects at Institute of Metal Research, CAS

Water vapor corrosion behaviors of rare-earth silicates environmental barrier coatings. Shenyang, China

Role: lead researcher Sept. 2023 – Ongoing

  • As-sprayed samples of rare-earth silicates coatings were annealed and then underwent water vapor corrosion tests at elevated temperature.

  • Extensive characterization was conducted using SEM/BSE-EDS, XRD, FIB-TEM techniques to investigate the effect of annealing and water vapor corrosion on the coatings’ properties.

  • Further image analysis was implemented on the SEM/BSE results to quantify the coatings’ properties. The evolution of cracks, porosity and TGO growth were studied.

Oxidation behavior of HEAs under static & cyclic high-temperature conditions. Shenyang, China

Role: lead researcher Nov. 2023 – Ongoing

  • Four typical HEAs were prepared and static/cyclic oxidation tests were carried out. Surface & cross-section SEM/BSE-EDS, oxidation kinetics, and XRD analysis were performed.

  • The effect of alloying elements and cyclic condition on the HT performance of alloys was studied.

Projects at Sino-European Institute of Aviation Engineering, CAUC

Electrochemical Migration (ECM) Behavior and Mechanism of PCB. Tianjin, China

Role: lead researcher Sept. 2021 – Jan. 2023

  • Designed and built a multi-functional ECM experimental setup to simulate the service conditions of electronic devices and perform various tests.

  • Conducted a thorough research on the impact of different surface finishes and environmental pollutants on the ECM process and its products and further published a relevant paper in the journal 'Journal of Electronic Materials'.

  • Built a numerical model to further investigate the ECM behavior utilizing numerical simulation method.

Research on the Construction of Automotive Corrosion Environmental Spectrum. Tianjin & Harbin, China

Role: researcher; coordinator Sept. 2020 – May 2022

  • Led research group members accomplishing extensive automotive corrosion tests and electrochemical tests.

  • Analyzed the raw data retrieved from field research and created a data collection system, acquired computer software right certificate in 2021.

  • Published a relevant paper as the student first author (assistant research professor as the first author) in the journal 'Engineering Failure Analysis' and applied the analysis process for national invention patent.

Research on automotive non-metallic aging based on China’s environment. Tianjin, China

Role: researcher July 2020 – Aug. 2021

  • Organized the analysis of the environmental aging data of specific cities in China.

  • Conducted relativity analytics, grey prediction and ArcGIS heat mapping on the laboratory accelerated and field exposure testing results.

  • Co-designed an analysis platform and acquired computer software right certificate in 2021.
